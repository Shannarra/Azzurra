Who are we?

Good food is food made from scratch from good ingredients. The better the ingredients the less cooking is required. Although the restaurant here is more internationally oriented, it is and will continue to be the traditions and fundamental cornerstones of Italian country cuisine that apply.

The menu contains a wide range of dishes created from quality products, produced and adapted to our kitchen.

Maurizio Quattromani opened Azzurra in Jens Bangs Gade. Maurizio had before that been through the chef and waiter training at the hotel school in Naples and worked at the Italian Navy. While working in London for a short period, he met a Danish girl who took him to Aalborg. Here Maurizio saw the possibilities in opening his own restaurant.

The Taste Of Azzurra

With roots in Italy, this is of course where you will find the original cuisine. There is a strong focus on maintaining the authentic Italian taste in appetizers, salads, pastas, main courses, menus and desserts. Emphasis is placed on a proper handling of the imported ingredients and a preparation that recreates the taste of how "Mamma" made it.

We opened Azzurra Nordkraft in the new cultural heart of Aalborg - Nordkraft. Following inquiries from Skråen, the owners of Azzurra agreed to follow these new facilities, in order to still be able to entertain Skråen's staff, crews, bands and musicians.

Caterings

Food is delivered to festive occasions - receptions, anniversaries, birthdays, company parties, meetings, family parties, etc. Some exciting, varied menus have been established, but if you have any special wishes, we will try to fulfill them to the best of our ability. In addition to the food, we also deliver wine that is tailored to the selected menus.
